DatosPsicologicos

Automated tool that generates and publishes short psychology fact videos.

What this app does

DatosPsicologicos is a content automation tool that creates short psychology videos, generates scripts, produces narration, renders the final video, and publishes it to TikTok.

The app uses TikTok Login Kit for account authorization and TikTok Content Posting API to upload and publish videos on authorized accounts.
